FAQ

Questions
answered honestly

Is it really free to start? +
Yes. The Starter plan is free with no credit card required. You get unlimited script generation, 20 hooks per product, 5 ad variations, caption export, and the ad scoring engine. There is no trial period — Starter stays free.
When will voice and avatar video be available? +
We're actively integrating ElevenLabs and HeyGen. We don't have a fixed launch date — we'd rather launch it right than rush it. Join the beta now and you'll be notified the moment it goes live, with free access as an early member.
What AI tools power Zuvlix? +
Claude AI (Anthropic) for script writing, hook generation, variation creation, and ad scoring. ElevenLabs for voice generation (coming soon). HeyGen for photorealistic avatar video (coming soon). All best-in-class tools — connected into one workflow.
How does the Shopify URL scraper work? +
Paste any product URL — Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon, or any site — and our AI reads the page and automatically extracts the product name, description, key benefits, target audience, and price. It also suggests the best ad angles and tone for that specific product. This saves you 5–10 minutes of briefing per video.
What is the Winning Ad Engine? +
It's our AI-powered ad analysis system. You paste your script and it scores the hook strength (0–100), predicts CTR and ROAS based on the platform and style, gives you a retention score, identifies your best target audience, and provides specific actionable improvements. Think of it as getting instant feedback from a 10-year paid media veteran — before you spend on production.
Can I use the scripts for paid ads? +
Yes. Scripts generated by Zuvlix are yours to use however you like — filming yourself, briefing a creator, or (when it launches) generating avatar video through the platform. Growth and Agency plan videos will be commercially licensed for paid advertising.
Is there a money-back guarantee? +
Yes. All paid plans include a 14-day full money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason within 14 days, contact us and we'll refund the full amount. No questions, no hassle.
