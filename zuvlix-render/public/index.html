WHY I BUILT ZUVLIX

I kept watching brilliant small business owners struggle to create video content — not because they didn't have great products, but because good video was expensive, slow, and completely unpredictable.

A single UGC creator costs $200–$500 per video. You brief them, wait a week, get something that misses the brief, ask for revisions, wait again. Meanwhile your competitor is running 20 variations a week with AI.

I built Zuvlix because the intelligence layer was missing. Creatify makes videos fast. But it doesn't tell you which angle will actually convert, what your competitor is doing, or score your hook before you spend on production.

That's the gap Zuvlix fills. Script intelligence + performance prediction + video production — in one platform, 60 seconds, starting at $0.
