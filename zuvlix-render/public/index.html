FAQ

QUESTIONS
ANSWERED.

Is it really free to start? +
Yes. Starter plan is free with no credit card required — forever. You get unlimited script generation, 20 hooks, 5 ad variations, caption export, and the ad scoring engine.
When will avatar video be available? +
We are integrating HeyGen now. We do not have a fixed date — we would rather launch it right than rush it. Join the beta and you will be first to know.
What AI tools power Zuvlix? +
Claude AI (Anthropic) for script writing, hook generation, variation creation, and ad scoring. ElevenLabs for voice — 9 voices on the free tier. HeyGen for avatar video — coming soon.
How does the custom avatar builder work? +
Go to the My Avatar tab. Describe your ideal presenter in plain English. AI generates a portrait preview, HeyGen and D-ID production brief, and rewrites your script to sound exactly like that person speaks.
Is there a money-back guarantee? +
Yes. All paid plans include a 14-day full money-back guarantee. Contact us within 14 days and we refund the full amount. No questions, no hassle.
Can I use the scripts for paid ads? +
Yes. Scripts are yours to use however you like — filming yourself, briefing a creator, or generating avatar video through the platform. Growth and Agency plan videos will be commercially licensed.
