FAQ

Questions
answered honestly

Is it really free to start? +
Yes. The Starter plan is free with no credit card required. You get unlimited script generation, 20 hooks, 5 ad variations, caption export, and the ad scoring engine. Starter stays free permanently.
When will avatar video be available? +
We are actively integrating HeyGen. We do not have a fixed launch date — we would rather launch it right than rush it. Join the beta now and you will be notified the moment it goes live, with free access as an early member.
What AI tools power Zuvlix? +
Claude AI (Anthropic) for script writing, hook generation, variation creation, and ad scoring. ElevenLabs for voice generation — 9 voices currently available on the free tier. HeyGen for photorealistic avatar video coming soon.
How does the custom avatar builder work? +
Go to the My Avatar tab in the app. Describe your ideal presenter in plain English — age, appearance, personality, setting. Our AI generates a portrait preview, a detailed HeyGen and D-ID production brief, and rewrites your script to sound exactly like that person speaks.
Is there a money-back guarantee? +
Yes. All paid plans include a 14-day full money-back guarantee. Contact us within 14 days if you are not satisfied and we will refund the full amount. No questions, no hassle.
